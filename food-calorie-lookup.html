About this data

All nutritional values are based on USDA FoodData Central data and represent averages per 100g of food as typically prepared (raw where noted, cooked otherwise for meats and grains). Values may vary slightly by brand, preparation method, and ripeness.

Use the serving size toggle to adjust values to a portion that matches your meal. For weight loss, focus on the calorie column alongside adequate protein intake to preserve muscle mass.

Lowest calorie foods

Per 100g: cucumber (15 kcal), lettuce (17 kcal), zucchini (17 kcal), tomato (18 kcal), spinach (23 kcal), strawberries (32 kcal).

High-volume, low-calorie foods help you feel full while maintaining a deficit.

Highest protein foods

Per 100g: whey protein (78g), chicken breast (31g), tuna (26g), peanuts (26g), turkey breast (30g), beef steak (30g).

Aim for 0.7–1g of protein per lb of bodyweight when losing weight to preserve muscle.
